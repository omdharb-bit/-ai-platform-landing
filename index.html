The engine

Everything a flow needs, in one surface

Hover a tile on desktop, or tap a row on mobile — the open panel follows you across either layout.

Drag sources, transforms and destinations onto an infinite canvas. Helix compiles the graph into a versioned, replayable pipeline automatically.

Change-data-capture keeps every destination within 120ms of the source. No cron, no drift.

Describe the output schema in plain English. Helix infers the mapping, writes the SQL, and flags every column it isn't sure about.

Statistical baselines per stream catch volume drops and schema breaks before they reach your dashboards.

Row-level policies, audit trails and SSO ship on day one. Grant access by role, not by spreadsheet.

Promote a flow from staging to production in one click. Live metrics, logs and lineage are wired up for every run with no extra config.
